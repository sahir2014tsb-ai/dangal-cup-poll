What is Dangal Cup?

Dangal Cup is the official proposed football tournament for the 7th grade of Shiv Nader School. It was created with one main purpose: to give 7th grade something legendary to call its own.

In Shiv Nader School, football culture already exists through iconic tournaments. The 9th grade has the famous Takkar Cup, known for fierce competition and unforgettable matches. The 11th grade has Pinde Cup, another respected tournament with prestige and reputation.

But despite all this, 7th grade never had a major football tournament of its own. That is where Dangal Cup comes in.

The Vision

Dangal Cup is designed to bring together competition, teamwork, strategy, leadership, and sportsmanship. It aims to create a real league experience inside 7th grade where every player matters.

This is not just about kicking a ball around. This is about representing your team, fighting for victory, and building your reputation. Every goal matters. Every save matters. Every match matters.

The Teams

The league will feature 8 teams inspired by famous football clubs:

  • Manchester City
  • Manchester United
  • Real Madrid
  • Barcelona
  • Arsenal
  • Chelsea
  • PSG
  • Tottenham

Each team will have:

  • A captain
  • A unique team identity
  • Players with defined roles
  • Its own reputation and legacy

Why It Matters

Dangal Cup is more than a tournament. It is an opportunity for 7th grade to build something memorable. Something people talk about. Something future grades remember.

This is competition. This is pride. This is legacy.

This is Dangal Cup ⚽
